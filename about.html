    About Us

    Around 2016 we noticed a gap in the hotelier and hospitality service. From our own experiences we noticed how much we appreciated an accommodation that gave us a “home away from home” feel but it was very rare that we found that. We knew that we wanted to offer an alternative to a hotel stay, accommodation that made people feel comfortable away from home. So we acquired our trusty units, made our homely additions and started short-term rentals.

    We noticed that our guests gave us the same feedback, they wanted a place for the whole family, instead of separate rooms. They wanted to cook their own meals and have a living room to relax in. The amenities of serviced accommodation made the industry more and more popular.


    At StayBNB, we’re not just a standard accommodation provider; we’re your partner in creating an exceptional stay for business professionals visiting Wolverhampton and the surrounding area. Our story began with a vision to redefine the concept of serviced accommodation, and over the years, we’ve evolved into a close-knit team dedicated to crafting comfortable and productive environments that cater to the unique needs of professionals.

    Our Commitment to Comfort and Productivity

    Our mission revolves around providing more than just a place to stay; we aim to offer a comfortable and productive haven that supports the demands of your professional journey. We understand that business travel can be demanding, which is why we’ve curated accommodations designed to offer the perfect blend of relaxation and efficiency. From thoughtfully furnished workspaces to high-speed internet connectivity, every aspect of your stay is meticulously designed to enhance your productivity while ensuring you have the opportunity to unwind in style.
